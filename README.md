# 📊 Superstore Sales Performance Analysis

## 🚀 End-to-End Business Intelligence Project  
**Tools Used:** Python (Pandas) | MySQL | Power BI  
**Author:** Aakash  

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs a complete end-to-end sales data analysis using real-world retail transaction data.  

The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that help answer key strategic questions such as:

- Which products generate the most revenue?
- Which categories are most profitable?
- How do sales change over time?
- Which regions perform best?
- Where should the business focus to grow faster?

This project simulates a real-world analytics workflow used in companies and consulting environments.

---

## 🛠️ Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning – Python (Pandas)

- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values
- Standardized categorical fields
- Converted date columns to datetime format
- Created derived fields:
  - Year
  - Month
  - Month Name
  - Profit Margin

The cleaned dataset was exported for database analysis.

---

### 2️⃣ Business Analysis – MySQL

Key SQL analysis performed:

- Total Revenue & Profit calculation
- Monthly Sales Trend analysis
- Category & Sub-Category performance
- Regional Sales & Profit comparison
- Top Revenue Generating Products
- Low Profit Margin product detection

This structured analysis helped uncover profitability gaps and growth opportunities.

---

### 3️⃣ Data Visualization – Power BI

An interactive dashboard was developed including:

✔ KPI Cards  
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Total Orders  
- Profit Margin %

✔ Sales Trend Over Time (Line Chart)  
✔ Category & Sub-Category Contribution  
✔ Regional Sales & Profit Analysis  
✔ Top Revenue Generating Products  
✔ Interactive Filters (Year, Segment, Region)

The dashboard enables stakeholders to explore performance dynamically.

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Technology category generates the highest revenue.
- Furniture shows strong sales but lower profit margins.
- West region leads in both revenue and profitability.
- Central region underperforms comparatively.
- Sales peak during Q4, indicating seasonal demand.
- Heavy discounting negatively impacts profit margins.
- A small number of products contribute significantly to total revenue.

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

- Focus marketing investment on high-margin categories.
- Re-evaluate discount strategies to protect profitability.
- Improve performance in underperforming regions.
- Increase inventory before seasonal peak periods.
- Review and optimize pricing for low-margin products.
